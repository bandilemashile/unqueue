FAQs

Do I need to provide any documents?

All you need is to fill in the required fields, and upload FICA documents for car renewals.

What are the costs?

Un-queue service fee is R160.00 per renewal (delivery fee included). The cost of the license disc is different for everyone and is calculated according to the weight of the car. The license disc fee is provided by the Licensing Authority.

What happens when my disc has expired already?

The Licencing Authority will add a penalty to the price of your disc which is 10% for each month that it is overdue. Unqueue will still renew your license. Your invoice will then include the license cost, the penalty and the service fee. Easy!

How long does it take?

From the time payment is made and received, it usually takes about 2 – 7 days for it to be delivered to your chosen delivery address.

What happens when I have fines to pay?

If you have fines that are blocking the renewal of your license, e.g. warrant of arrest or enforcement order, the client needs to pay the fines before the renewal can be issued.

I lost my license disc do I have to pay the full amount again to receive a new one?

No, you only pay a pro-rata fee, the date from which the disk was issued until the current date. You only pay for the time you actually had the disk. Now when you license your vehicle you’ll do it as if you do not have the renewal reminder. The new expiry date will be a year from the current date.
